Rodellar 2010

First time visit of Rodellar. A perfect climbing area. All the cliffs are close in walking distance. The 'Riglos' are not far away - so you have to go there and climb 'Fiesta del Biceps'. The problem with the 'Biceps' is not really the power - rather the endurance. You get really really tired - especially when climbing with a backpack - uff.
Team: Dorthe, Rainer, Lexi, Romy, Reto and a bunch of niiiice spanish guys (gäll Romy ;-)
